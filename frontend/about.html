About YTCuttr

A free, fast, and simple tool built for anyone who wants to save exactly the right moment from a YouTube video — without downloading the whole thing.

"Save the moment that matters."

YTCuttr exists because downloading an 8-hour stream just to get a 2-minute highlight is frustrating. We fixed that.

💡 Why We Built YTCuttr

YouTube is full of incredible content — but sometimes you only need a small piece of it. A key moment from a lecture. A highlight from a 6-hour gaming stream. A recipe step from a long cooking video. A soundbite from a 2-hour podcast.

Most tools either require you to download the entire video first, add ugly watermarks, or force you to sign up and pay. We found that frustrating — so we built YTCuttr.

With YTCuttr, you paste a URL, set your start and end timestamps, choose quality, and download just the clip you need. That's it. Fast, clean, and free.
