Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

🔒 Short version: We do not collect, store, or share your personal data or videos. YTCuttr processes your clip on our server and deletes it immediately after you download it. No account needed. No tracking beyond basic analytics.

📋 What This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy explains how YTCuttr ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our website at ytcuttr.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

📦 Information We Do NOT Collect

YTCuttr is designed to be privacy-first. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal information
  • Account credentials (there are no accounts)
  • The YouTube URLs you paste into our tool
  • The video clips or audio files you download
  • Payment information (the tool is free)

🎬 How Video Processing Works

When you use YTCuttr to cut or download a YouTube clip:

  • Your request is sent to our processing server
  • The clip is generated using the timestamps you provided
  • The file is delivered to your browser for download
  • The file is permanently deleted from our server immediately after delivery

We do not retain, archive, or analyse any video content you process through YTCuttr.

📊 Analytics & Cookies

We may use basic analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand how many people visit our site and which features are used. This data is:

  • Aggregated and anonymous — we cannot identify individual users
  • Used only to improve the tool and fix issues
  • Never sold or shared with third parties for advertising

Standard browser cookies may be set by analytics tools. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

🤝 Third-Party Services

YTCuttr uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Netlify — Frontend hosting (ytcuttr.com)
  • Railway.app — Backend processing server
  • Google Analytics — Anonymous usage statistics

Each of these services has their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them independently. We do not share your personal data with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

👦 Children's Privacy

YTCuttr is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used our service and provided personal data, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of YTCuttr after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
